The Faded Page transcription is of a 1960 edition that was “reset” to restore some of Waugh’s original text that was removed by his first publisher. The text of this edition was updated before the initial commit to remove the changes made in that 1960 edition to keep any copyrighted text out of the repository.

The thematic breaks were missing from the transcription and were added manually.

The first (universally available) IA scan is missing all periods, including those in colons, question marks, and exclamation points (and possibly some/most semi-colons), but was used to proofread the text. The second (one-hour borrow) IA scan was used to proofread the punctuation.
